About Me

I am a data enthusiast with a background in Industrial and Applied Chemistry and a strong passion for data science. My journey has been fueled by curiosity—starting in the lab, analyzing chemical data, and now exploring insights from large datasets.

Most of my time is spent working with data—cleaning, analyzing, and building predictive models. My skills include data analytics, visualization, machine learning, and predictive modeling.
